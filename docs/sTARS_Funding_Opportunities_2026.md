**Compiled:** February 3, 2026

**Purpose:** Identify competitions, grants, and funding for sTARS modular space infrastructure platform

## Executive Summary

The sTARS platform's modular architecture, closed-loop life support, and integrated food production capabilities position it for multiple funding streams beyond the Mars to Table Challenge. This document outlines **14+ active opportunities** totaling potential funding of **$10M+** across NASA, NSF, ARPA-E, USDA, and private sector programs.

## Tier 1: HIGH PRIORITY (Direct Alignment with sTARS)

### 1. NASA LunaRecycle Challenge - Phase 2
**Prize Pool:** $3,000,000 ($2M in Phase 2)
**Deadline:** January 22, 2026 (Milestone), August 2026 (Finals)
**Status:** ACCEPTING SUBMISSIONS NOW

**Relevance:** sTARS waste processing POD directly addresses this challenge's goals - converting fabrics, plastics, foam, and metals into useful resources.

**Details:**
- Focus on recycling solutions for lunar missions
- Received 1,200+ registrations (most in Centennial Challenges history)
- Up to 20 finalists selected February 2026
- In-person demonstrations August 2026
- Only U.S. teams eligible for Phase 2

**Our Angle:** Our Waste Processing POD (anaerobic digestion, biogas SOFC, pyrolysis) and closed-loop nutrient cycling are directly applicable.

**Source:** [NASA LunaRecycle Challenge](https://www.nasa.gov/prizes-challenges-and-crowdsourcing/centennial-challenges/lunarecycle/)

### 2. NASA Human Lander Challenge 2026 (ECLSS Focus)
**Prize Pool:** $18,000 (top 3) + $9,000 stipends (up to 12 finalists)
**Deadline:** Phase 1 proposals due early 2026
**Status:** OPEN

**Relevance:** Focus on Environmental Control and Life Support System (ECLSS) - air, water, and waste systems for lunar landers.

**Details:**
- Undergraduate and graduate teams with faculty advisors
- Systems-level solutions for ECLSS performance
- Final design review June 23-25, 2026 at Marshall Space Flight Center
- Supports Artemis campaign landers (SpaceX, Blue Origin)

**Our Angle:** Partner with university team as industry mentor, contribute sTARS ECLSS integration expertise.

**Source:** [NASA Human Lander Challenge](https://www.nasa.gov/directorates/esdmd/artemis-campaign-development-division/human-landing-system-program/human-lander-challenge/nasa-opens-2026-human-lander-challenge-for-life-support-systems-more/)

**Deadline:** Products due May 2026
**Status:** ACTIVE

### 4. NASA SBIR/STTR Program Year 2026
**Funding:** Phase I: $150,000 | Phase II: $850,000 | CCRPP: up to $2.5M match
**Deadline:** TBD (awaiting BAA release and program reauthorization)
**Status:** PENDING - Legislative action required

**Relevance:** Primary small business R&D funding for NASA-relevant technologies.

**Details:**

**Our Angle:** Propose sTARS POD architecture, RSV systems, SEP protection as subtopic solutions.
